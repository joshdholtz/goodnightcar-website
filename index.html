Tuck your vehicle into bed with ease

Tap the map, click register, and done - GoodnightCar is the best way to register your car with Milwaukee's Night Parking Permission website.

Forget the clunky interfaces

GoodnightCar is built specifically for today's mobile devices. There is no need to navigate through a website designed for your desktop.

Simply tap where you're at

You don't need to know the address where you are parked at. Simply tap on the map and we will tell you the address you are at.

We remember your licence plate

License plates are the dickens to remember. But no worries, we're able to take care of that. Once you type in your license plate we will remember it and present it to you the next time you open the app.

Never forget how many registrations you have left

Your car is only allowed to register for night parking 3 nights in a 30 day period in Milwaukee. GoodnightCar keeps a history of all the times you have registered so you will never have to question again if you used up your 3 nights of parking in the last 30 days.
